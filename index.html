Works with your current music library

It's very easy to switch from iTunes to SLC -- without any copying. Just point SLC to your music and video folders and those files will appear. If you don't like SLC (impossible!), nothing's changed.

Converts and syncs to Android & Kindle Fire

You'd have to be crazy to use a music player that doesn't sync to your phone. SLC is simply the best music and video player for Android phones and tablets.

Buy Music and Apps inside SLC

The Amazon MP3 store is built-in to SLC. Buy, download, and listen, seamlessly. Buy Android apps from the Amazon or Google app stores and they will sync to your device.
